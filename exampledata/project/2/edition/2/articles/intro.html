Introduction

The hurdy-gurdy is one of the stringed instruments that originated in the Middle Ages. It is a mechanical instrument with a unique structure and a distinctive tone. 

Although it has not become a major instrument in European classical music, it is very important in European folk music, especially dance music. By learning about the hurdy-gurdy, you will be able to experience a more folkloric musical culture that is different from the so-called "Western classical music."
